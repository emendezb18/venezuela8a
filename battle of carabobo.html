Battle of Carabobo

battle of Carabobo happened on June 24, 1821 and ended with a fought victory of the patriots.In the battle of Carabobo two sides fought.The patriot army, which was led by Simon Bolivar and Jose Antonio Paez in representation of Gran Colombia.The realistic army, which was led by the Spanish Miguel de la Torre that represented the Spanish Royal Empire.The meeting took place in the savanna of Carabobo, and the important thing about this battle was that with this triumph the patriots managed to free Venezuela from the Spanish empire.In this battle around 3,000 patriotic warriors died. This event must be remembered by people because with this battle they were released from a Spanish mandate that lasted a long time.
