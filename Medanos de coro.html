Medanos de Coro National Park

Médanos de Coro National park , Why is it called Medanos de coro ?Because the notion of dune comes from the Hispanic Arabic maydan. The term refers to a dune: an elevation of sand that the wind is responsible for forming.This national park is located in falcón state in venezuela , This beautiful park is made up of sand ,the dunes are approximately 20 meters high, The temperature in this park is approximately 28.2°C. The better activity to do in this beautiful natural park is sand board , It is an activity similar to skiing but with boards made for the sand ,some animals that can be found in this place are wild rabbits, the pygmy anteater, the arboreal porcupine, the desert marmosa and the Paraguaná mustached bat.
