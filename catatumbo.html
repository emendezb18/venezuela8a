Catatumbo river

Catatumbo river the rivers name means House of Thunders because occur a lightnings storm every 300 days in the catatumbo and lake maracaibo is connected , This river is rising in the northern of colombia and it flow in the northeast across of venezuela border and connect with the lake Maracaibo.This beautiful river have approximately 340 kilometers long .Its forms part of the nacional internacional boundary of the two countries.Catatumbo ia a producing area of cacao beans.
