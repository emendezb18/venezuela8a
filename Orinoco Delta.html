Orinoco Delta

In the orinoco delta they try to protect the animals that live in that important place between Venezuela and Guyana and the Warao tribe is in charge of that.The river it has is one of the largest in the world that has a great variety of animals such as Crocodiles, Piranha, Snakes, among others. Speaking of the tribe, the Warao people live on the banks of the pipes or arms that form the Orinoco Delta, Delta Amacuro state. In their traditions it is estimated that they were already there when the island of Trinidad was attached to the mainland, that is, approximately between 8,500 and 9,000 years ago.
