Orinoco River

mostly all of Venezuela and east of Colombia, you will find a very important flow of water named the Orinoco river. This ancient body of water is one of the biggest rivers in South America. This legend has many mineral deposits such as iron ore, chrome, bauxite, vanadium, nickel, and manganese. The Piaroa and the warao have used this body of water for different things, these indigenous people who live in the interior of Venezuela have long used the Orinoco and its tributaries as large waterways.to sustain the traditional way of life of the indigenous tribe of the piaroa for a thousand years. Women grow food and the men fish. For tourists, you can try and swim in the Orinoco river. The water is warm so it is very enjoyable. Also you can try finding animals like howler monkeys. In Addition to that you can also enjoy a boat or canoe trip. You will surely like this experience !
