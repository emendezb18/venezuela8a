Hugo Chavez Presidency

After Chavez died, Nicolas Maduro was elected as president of Venezuela in 2013. As the year progressed, growth of the Venezuelan economy slowed to a crawl, inflation climbed above 50 percent, and things such as toilet paper, milk, and flour became increasingly difficult to obtain.
