Morocoy National Park

The park is one of the most emblematic national parks of Venezuela, due to its large size, its recreation areas, hotels and its incredible sea, this park was declared a national park in 1974, other of its main tourists are those who practice diving due to its large number of steep rocks.Another thing that attracts tourists is its great flora and fauna, the park is characterized by showing strange animals and flowers. The park also offers diving with the fish and crossing to different beaches by kayak, generally people like the beach because of its light color and its large size. The importance of this park is to protect the beaches, keys, landscapes and, above all, the animals that are in danger of extinction, such as the pink dolphin.
