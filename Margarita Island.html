Margarita Island

In the north of Venezuela we are going to find the historical Margarita Island. Margarita is well known for the quality of its beaches and the amount of pearls surrounding the island made it famous very quickly. This island's income includes the building of fishing boats, tourism and the manufacture of tile, ceramics, shoes, hats, and as mentioned , it is surrounded by beaches, and there is one for everything you like. You can find the beach you want, windy, with big waves or small ones etc. Margarita Island was first discovered by Christopher Columbus in 1498. The island was initially settled by the Indigenous Guaiqueries people. This island was also used by simon bolivar in the war with spain as a base of operations in 1816. The easiest way to get to Margarita island is by airplane. travelers can get to Margarita Island by flying into Porlamar Airport in the state of Nueva Esparta. The airport is located right on Margarita Island.
