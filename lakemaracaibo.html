Lake Maracaibo

Lake Maracaibo is located in the northwest of Venezuela. This beautiful lake is one of the largest natural lakes of South America. Lake Maracaibo is considered a lake and a bay because it is connected with the ocean through a 55 kilometer strait that joins it to the Gulf of Venezuela.
